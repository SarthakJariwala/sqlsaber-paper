Based on your requirements, I will join the `drivers` and `pitStops` tables to calculate each driver's average pit stop duration, filtering specifically for German drivers born between the years 1980 and 1985.

Here is the SQL query used to find the result:

```sql
SELECT 
    d.forename || ' ' || d.surname AS full_name,
    SUM(CAST(p.duration AS REAL)) / COUNT(p.duration) AS average_pitstop_duration
FROM drivers d
JOIN pitStops p ON d.driverId = p.driverId
WHERE d.nationality = 'German'
  AND CAST(strftime('%Y', d.dob) AS INTEGER) BETWEEN 1980 AND 1985
GROUP BY d.driverId
ORDER BY average_pitstop_duration ASC
LIMIT 3;
```
