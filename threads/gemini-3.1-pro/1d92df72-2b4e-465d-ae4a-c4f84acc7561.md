```sql
SELECT
  COUNT(T1.ID)
FROM Patient AS T1
JOIN Examination AS T2
  ON T1.ID = T2.ID
WHERE
  CAST(STRFTIME('%Y', T2.`Examination Date`) AS INTEGER) BETWEEN 1990 AND 1993
  AND CAST(STRFTIME('%Y', T2.`Examination Date`) AS INTEGER) - CAST(STRFTIME('%Y', T1.Birthday) AS INTEGER) < 18;
```

- **`CAST(...) - CAST(...) < 18`**: This computes the age of the patient at the time they were examined by subtracting their birth year from the examination year, and filters to include only those who were under 18 years old.
- **`COUNT(T1.ID)`**: Finally, this counts the total number of patient records that match all the conditions above.
