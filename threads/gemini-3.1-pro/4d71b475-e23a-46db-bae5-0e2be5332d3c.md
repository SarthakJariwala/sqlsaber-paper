To find out which away team won the most matches in the Scotland Premier League during the 2009/2010 season, I explored the match records, filtering by the specific league and season, and then counted the wins for each team when playing as 
the away team.

Here is the query I used to get the answer:
```sql
SELECT
  T.team_long_name
FROM League AS L
JOIN Match AS M ON L.id = M.league_id
JOIN Team AS T ON M.away_team_api_id = T.team_api_id
WHERE L.name = 'Scotland Premier League'
  AND M.season = '2009/2010'
  AND M.away_team_goal > M.home_team_goal
GROUP BY T.team_api_id, T.team_long_name
ORDER BY COUNT(*) DESC
LIMIT 1;
```

The away team that won the most matches during the 2009/2010 season of the Scotland Premier League was **Rangers**.

- The query connects the `League`, `Match`, and `Team` tables together to get the full details of the games and teams.
- It narrows down the records to only look at matches in the "Scotland Premier League" during the "2009/2010" season.
- It isolates away wins by checking if the away team scored more goals than the home team (`away_team_goal > home_team_goal`).
- Finally, it groups the results by team, counts the number of away wins for each, sorts them from highest to lowest, and returns the team at the top of the list.
