To find the percentage of female superheroes who have blue skin out of all female superheroes, I will examine the `superhero` table along with the `gender` and `colour` tables. 

Here is the query I used to calculate the percentage:

```sql
SELECT 
  SUM(CASE WHEN c.colour = 'Blue' THEN 1.0 ELSE 0 END) * 100.0 / COUNT(s.id) AS percentage_blue_female
FROM superhero s
JOIN gender g ON s.gender_id = g.id
LEFT JOIN colour c ON s.skin_colour_id = c.id
WHERE g.gender = 'Female';
```

### Explanation of the Query:
- **`JOIN gender g`**: We match the superheroes to their gender.
- **`LEFT JOIN colour c`**: We link the superheroes to their skin colors. 
- **`WHERE g.gender = 'Female'`**: We restrict the list to only female superheroes.
- **`SUM(CASE ... )`**: This counts how many of those female superheroes specifically have 'Blue' as their skin color.
- **`* 100.0 / COUNT(s.id)`**: We divide the number of blue female superheroes by the total number of female superheroes and multiply by 100 to get a percentage.

Approximately **2.46%** of all female superheroes have blue skin (which accounts for 5 blue-skinned female superheroes out of 203 total female superheroes).
