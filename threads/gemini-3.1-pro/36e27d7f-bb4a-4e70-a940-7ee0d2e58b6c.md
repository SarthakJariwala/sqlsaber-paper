Here is the SQL query used to find the percentage:

```sql
SELECT 
  CAST(SUM(CASE WHEN T2.publisher_name = 'Marvel Comics' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS REAL) * 100 / COUNT(T2.id) AS marvel_percentage
FROM superhero T1
LEFT JOIN publisher T2 
  ON T1.publisher_id = T2.id
WHERE T1.height_cm BETWEEN 150 AND 180;
```

* **`WHERE T1.height_cm BETWEEN 150 AND 180`**: This restricts our data to only look at superheroes whose height is between 150 and 180 centimeters.
* **`SUM(CASE WHEN T2.publisher_name = 'Marvel Comics' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END)`**: This acts as a tally, adding "1" every time we see a hero published by Marvel. 
* **`COUNT(T2.id)`**: This counts the total number of heroes in this height range who have a known publisher.
* **`CAST(...) * 100 / ...`**: Finally, this calculates the fraction of Marvel heroes out of the total and multiplies by 100 to convert it into a percentage format.
