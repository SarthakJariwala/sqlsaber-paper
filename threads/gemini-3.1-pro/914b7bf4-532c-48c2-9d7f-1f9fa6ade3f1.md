4-digit birth year from the patient's `Birthday`.

- **`WHERE T2.GLU >= 180 AND T2.\`T-CHO\` < 250`**: Filters the results to strictly include patients who exhibit elevated blood glucose values (not normal) alongside standard total cholesterol figures (normal). Backticks are used around
