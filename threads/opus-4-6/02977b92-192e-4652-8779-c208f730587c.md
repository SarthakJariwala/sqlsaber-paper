**Logic:**
1. Join `Patient` and `Laboratory` tables on `ID`
2. Filter for patients **older than 55** using `Birthday`
3. Filter for **abnormal PT** (PT >= 14)
4. Calculate the **percentage of females** among those with abnormal PT


**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
  CAST(SUM(CASE WHEN P.SEX = 'F' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS REAL) * 100 / COUNT(*) AS percentage_female
FROM Patient P
INNER JOIN Laboratory L ON P.ID = L.ID
WHERE (strftime('%Y', 'now') - strftime('%Y', P.Birthday)) > 55
  AND L.PT >= 14;

```
