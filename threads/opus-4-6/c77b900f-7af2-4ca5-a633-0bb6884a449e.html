Let me run the query to find leagues where the average home team goals exceed the average away team goals in the 2009/2010 season. **What this query does:** It joins the `Match` and `League` tables, filters for the `2009/2010` season, groups by league, and then compares the average home goals vs. average away goals per league — keeping only those leagues where home teams scored more on average.
Assistant
⚡ execute_sql 

SELECT L.name AS League_Name
FROM Match M
INNER JOIN League L ON M.league_id = L.id
WHERE M.season = '2009/2010'
GROUP BY L.name
HAVING CAST(SUM(M.home_team_goal) AS REAL) / COUNT(DISTINCT M.id) > CAST(SUM(M.away_team_goal) AS REAL) / COUNT(DISTINCT M.id)
