Now I have a clear picture of the database. Let me query for the constructor with the most points from the Monaco Grand Prix between 1980 and 2010.


**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    SUM(cr.points) AS total_points,
    c.name AS constructor_name,
    c.nationality
FROM constructorResults cr
JOIN races r ON cr.raceId = r.raceId
JOIN constructors c ON cr.constructorId = c.constructorId
WHERE r.name LIKE '%Monaco%'
  AND r.year BETWEEN 1980 AND 2010
GROUP BY cr.constructorId, c.name, c.nationality
ORDER BY total_points DESC
LIMIT 5;

```
