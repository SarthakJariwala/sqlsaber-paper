**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT P.ID) AS patient_count
FROM Patient P
INNER JOIN Laboratory L ON P.ID = L.ID
WHERE L.CRE >= 1.5
  AND (STRFTIME('%Y', 'now') - STRFTIME('%Y', P.Birthday)) < 70;

```
