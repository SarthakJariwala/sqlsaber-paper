Now I have a clear picture of the schema. Let me write and execute the query to find the away team with the most wins in the Scotland Premier League during the 2009/2010 season.

**Query Logic:**
- Join `Match` with `League` to filter for **Scotland Premier League**
- Join `Match` with `Team` to get the **away team's full name**
- Filter for the **2009/2010 season**
- An away win means `away_team_goal > home_team_goal`
- Count the away wins per team and return the team with the **most away wins**


**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    T.team_long_name,
    COUNT(*) AS away_wins
FROM Match M
JOIN League L ON M.league_id = L.id
JOIN Team T ON M.away_team_api_id = T.team_api_id
WHERE L.name = 'Scotland Premier League'
  AND M.season = '2009/2010'
  AND M.away_team_goal > M.home_team_goal
GROUP BY T.team_long_name
ORDER BY away_wins DESC
LIMIT 5;

```
