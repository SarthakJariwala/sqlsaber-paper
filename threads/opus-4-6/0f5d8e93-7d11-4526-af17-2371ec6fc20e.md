**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    P.ID,
    P.Birthday,
    P."First Date",
    L.Date AS Lab_Date,
    (STRFTIME('%Y', P."First Date") - STRFTIME('%Y', P.Birthday)) AS Age_At_First_Visit
FROM Patient P
INNER JOIN Laboratory L ON P.ID = L.ID
WHERE P.Diagnosis = 'SJS'
ORDER BY P.Birthday ASC
LIMIT 1;

```
