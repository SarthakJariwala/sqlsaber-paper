**Goal:** Among all bonds belonging to **carcinogenic molecules** (label = '+'), find what **percentage** are **triple bonds** (bond_type = '#').


**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    CAST(SUM(CASE WHEN b.bond_type = '#' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) * 100.0 / COUNT(b.bond_id) AS REAL) AS percent
FROM bond b
JOIN molecule m ON b.molecule_id = m.molecule_id
WHERE m.label = '+'
```
