**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    CAST(SUM(CASE WHEN g.gender = 'Female' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS REAL) * 100.0 / COUNT(*) AS percentage_female
FROM superhero s
JOIN publisher p ON s.publisher_id = p.id
JOIN gender g ON s.gender_id = g.id
WHERE p.publisher_name = 'George Lucas';

```
