**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT P.ID) AS underage_patient_count
FROM Patient P
JOIN Examination E ON P.ID = E.ID
WHERE STRFTIME('%Y', E.`Examination Date`) BETWEEN '1990' AND '1993'
  AND (STRFTIME('%Y', E.`Examination Date`) - STRFTIME('%Y', P.Birthday)) < 18;

```
