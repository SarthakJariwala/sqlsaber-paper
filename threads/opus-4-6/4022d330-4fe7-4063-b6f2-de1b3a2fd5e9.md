Now I have a clear picture of the schema. Let me write and execute the query:

- **Patient** table has `ID` and `Birthday`
- **Laboratory** table has `ID` and `HCT` (hematocrit level)
- Age is calculated as: `current year - birth year`
- We need patients with **more than 2** lab records where `HCT >= 52`


**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    P.ID,
    (STRFTIME('%Y', 'now') - STRFTIME('%Y', P.Birthday)) AS age
FROM Patient P
INNER JOIN Laboratory L ON P.ID = L.ID
WHERE L.HCT >= 52
GROUP BY P.ID
HAVING COUNT(P.ID) > 2

```
