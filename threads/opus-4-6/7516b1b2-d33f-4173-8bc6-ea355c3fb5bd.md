Now I have a clear picture of the schema. Here's the plan:

- **`cards`** has `name`, `setCode`, and `uuid`
- **`sets`** has `name` and `code` (links to `cards.setCode`)
- **`legalities`** has `format`, `status`, and `uuid` (foreign key to `cards.uuid`)

I'll join these three tables to get all cards from the "Hour of Devastation" set along with the formats where they are **Legal**.


**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT DISTINCT
    c.name AS card_name,
    l.format
FROM cards c
JOIN sets s ON c.setCode = s.code
JOIN legalities l ON c.uuid = l.uuid
WHERE s.name = 'Hour of Devastation'
  AND l.status = 'Legal'
ORDER BY c.name, l.format;

```
