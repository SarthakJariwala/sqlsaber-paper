**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT forename || ' ' || surname AS full_name
FROM drivers d
JOIN qualifying q ON d.driverId = q.driverId
JOIN races r ON q.raceId = r.raceId
JOIN circuits c ON r.circuitId = c.circuitId
WHERE c.name = 'Marina Bay Street Circuit'
  AND r.year = 2008
  AND q.q3 IS NOT NULL
  AND q.q3 != ''
ORDER BY q.q3 ASC
LIMIT 1;

```
