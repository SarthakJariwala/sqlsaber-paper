**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT 
    SUM(CASE WHEN e.event_name = 'Yearly Kickoff' THEN b.amount END) * 1.0 
    / SUM(CASE WHEN e.event_name = 'October Meeting' THEN b.amount END) AS times_more
FROM budget b
JOIN event e ON b.link_to_event = e.event_id
WHERE b.category = 'Advertisement'
  AND e.event_name IN ('Yearly Kickoff', 'October Meeting')

```
