**Executing SQL:**

```sql

SELECT DISTINCT
    e.event_name,
    e.event_date
FROM event e
JOIN budget b ON b.link_to_event = e.event_id
JOIN expense ex ON ex.link_to_budget = b.budget_id
WHERE ex.expense_description = 'Pizza'
  AND ex.cost > 50
  AND ex.cost < 100;

```
